## Welcome!

I joined Deis Robotics in Fall 2023 with the belief that robotics wasn't for me. I had very little background in anything engineering-related, and most of this knowledge did not come to me easily.

This project is a compilation of everything I have learned, from measuring a screw to designing a full working robot. I am trying to make the beginner friendly resource that I needed back then, for those who are truly starting from the bottom.
